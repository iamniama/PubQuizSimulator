ONLINE PUB QUIZ SIMULATOR

HOW TO PLAY

  • Click your guess beneath the question
  • Speed is a factor, the point value of the question gets lower as time goes by
  • A wrong answer will cost you points, but not as much as not answering
  • You may also click PASS, to surrender the question without losing points
  • You will be competing against three teams: one weak, one average, and one with advantages
  • There is a pause between rounds, click READY to advance to the next round.
  • Click this panel to close it, then click READY and get on with the game!
  • Refresh the page to play again!
